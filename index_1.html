Purpose:

Purpose: You are being asked to take part in a research study. This research study aims to evaluate onboarding newcomers' process to a project and understand how to make onboarding easier for the newcomers to a project. Usually, after a newcomer finds a project to contribute, he has to decide what issue he can solve. Issues are problems or new needs reported by users, clients, or even the project's maintainers.

This experiment is composed of two steps:

  • 1) Select the issues you have the best skills to contribute to.

  • 2) Answer a brief survey reporting your experience.



Consent:

  • Activities: We will present you with a page with a list of issues, and you will select three of them in your order of preference to start a contribution. After you will fill a survey reporting your experience.

  • Time: Your participation in this study is expected to last up to 30 minutes.

  • Confidentiality: It is possible that others could learn that you participated in this study, but the information you provide will be kept confidential to the extent permitted by law.

  • Voluntary: Participation in this study is voluntary. You are free to decide not to participate or withdraw at any time without adversely affecting your relationship with the investigators.



Instructions:

  • The menu option "begin" shows a list of issues from the JabRef project. You are going to select three issues to start contributing. You can navigate from the initial page to one with the details of the issue by selecting the link on the issue title.

  • Use the information available and your set of skills to choose the best three issues in your preference order. Take notes of the relevant information that helps you to select these issues and the order of preference.

  • Please, do not navigate to outside of the main page and the issues specific pages. Use only those pages to decide whether or not you want to contribute to such an issue.

  • When you FINISH, please, Fill the survey on the menu option "survey," explaining why you chose those three (3) issues to contribute. Emphasize why do you think that those tasks match with your skill profile.
